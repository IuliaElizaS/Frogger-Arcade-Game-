Wellcome to the Ice Age style FROGGER!

Please help Scratt cross the stones and reach the Mountain. On his way he must not enter in collision with Sid, Buck or Diego. Achorns can be collected by Scratt, but they are counted only if he reaches the mountain with the achorn.

You have 3 minutes and 3 lives to collect as many achorns as possible.

To move Scratt you have to use the arrow keys.

Game End!
